Explore Dublin

Experience the freedom of exploring Dublin on two wheels. Our bike-sharing service provides a convenient, eco-friendly way to navigate the city's historic streets and vibrant neighborhoods. Whether you're commuting to work or touring the city's attractions, our bikes are available 24/7 at numerous stations throughout Dublin.

Easy to Use

Our bike-sharing system is designed with simplicity in mind. Simply locate a nearby station using our real-time map, unlock a bike with your membership card or mobile app, and start your journey. With flexible rental options and affordable rates, getting around Dublin has never been more convenient.
